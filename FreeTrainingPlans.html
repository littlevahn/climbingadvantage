A Few Notes on How to Approach This Program

The weekly structure is designed as follows: 2 days on, 1 day off, 2 days on, 2 days off.
A schedule I like looks like this: Train on Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday.
Of course, feel free to adjust the days to fit your personal schedule — consistency is what matters most.

Always, always, always warm up before each session.
Your warm-up isn’t just about injury prevention — it sets the tone for your training.
Move slowly and with intention. Climb with precision and control.
Dial in your footwork. Be mindful of your hips.
The focus you bring to your warm-up will carry through your entire session.
